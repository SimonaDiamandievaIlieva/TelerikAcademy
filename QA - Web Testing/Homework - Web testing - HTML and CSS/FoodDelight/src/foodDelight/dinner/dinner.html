A little info about the author: Hello, everyone. My name is Simona, and I've been a master chef for about two days (starting from the date of the HTML challenge). Today, I am delighted to present to you the fanciest dinner I am capable of creating. Aaaand the winner is ... French Fried Potatoes

The best potatoes for French fries are soaked in a sugar solution before frying. The sugar solution has something to do with the carbohydrates and prevents the potatoes from soaking up a lot of grease, so they get crunchy.

Here are the ingredients:

  • * 2 cups warm water
  • * â…“ cup white sugar
  • * 2 large russet potatoes - peeled, and sliced into 1/4 inch strips
  • * 6 cups vegetable oil for frying
  • * salt to taste

Instructions:

  1. Step 1:
    Stir together warm water and sugar in a medium bowl. Soak potatoes in water mixture for 15 minutes. Remove potatoes from water and dry thoroughly on paper towels.
  2. Step 2:
    Heat oil in a deep-fryer to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  3. Step 3:
    Cook potatoes in hot oil until golden, 5 to 6 minutes. Drain on paper towels and season with salt.
  4. Step 4:
    Eat everything you cooked, drink a beer and enjoy life.
